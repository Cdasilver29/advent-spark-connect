## Design Notes for Canva

### Recommended Color Palette (SDA Brand Colors):
- **Primary Blue**: #1E3A5F (Navy/Deep Blue)
- **Gold/Amber**: #D4A84B (Accent color)
- **White**: #FFFFFF (Background/Text)
- **Soft Cream**: #FFF8E7 (Warm backgrounds)

### Recommended Fonts:
- **Headlines**: Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond (Elegant serif)
- **Body Text**: Open Sans or Lato (Clean sans-serif)

### Image Suggestions:
- African-American couples in formal/semi-formal settings
- Church or elegant venue backgrounds
- Heart imagery and wedding elements
- Group activity photos
- Professional, warm, inclusive imagery

### Slide Layout Tips:
1. Use consistent margins and spacing
2. Limit text per slide (max 6 bullet points)
3. Include visuals on 80% of slides
4. Use icons to represent concepts
5. Maintain brand colors throughout
6. Add subtle animations for presentation flow
